Our Story

A legacy of exceptional dental care, built on trust and compassion

The Beginning

Over 20 years ago, Dr. Rosario Alvarez had a vision: to create a dental practice that would treat every patient like family. With this dream in mind, she opened the doors to Alvarez Dental Group in the heart of Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Starting with just one dental chair and a commitment to excellence, Dr. Alvarez built her practice on the principles of gentle care, advanced technology, and personalized attention. Word quickly spread throughout the Cambridge community about the exceptional care patients received at Alvarez Dental Group.

Growing with the Community

As the practice grew, so did our team of dedicated dental professionals. We expanded our services to meet the evolving needs of our patients, incorporating the latest advancements in dental technology while maintaining the warm, personal touch that has always been our hallmark.

Today, Alvarez Dental Group serves thousands of Cambridge families, from young children experiencing their first dental visit to seniors maintaining their smiles for life. We've had the privilege of caring for multiple generations of families, watching children grow into adults and then bringing their own children to us for care.
