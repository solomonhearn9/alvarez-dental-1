Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our advanced cosmetic procedures

Create the Smile of Your Dreams

Your smile is one of the first things people notice about you. At Alvarez Dental Group, we combine artistry with advanced dental techniques to create beautiful, natural-looking results that boost your confidence and enhance your appearance.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Achieve a brighter, more radiant smile with our professional teeth whitening treatments. Our advanced whitening systems deliver dramatic results that are safe, effective, and long-lasting.

  • In-office whitening for immediate results
  • Take-home whitening kits for convenience
  • Custom-fitted whitening trays
  • Results that last with proper maintenance
  • Safe for tooth enamel and gums

Porcelain Veneers

Transform the appearance of your smile with ultra-thin porcelain veneers. These custom-crafted shells cover the front of your teeth, correcting imperfections and creating a flawless, natural-looking smile.

  • Correct chips, cracks, and gaps
  • Improve tooth color and shape
  • Long-lasting and stain-resistant
  • Natural-looking translucent appearance
  • Minimal tooth preparation required

Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a quick and affordable way to improve the appearance of your teeth. We use tooth-colored composite resin to repair chips, close gaps, and reshape teeth for a more attractive smile.

  • Completed in a single visit
  • Color-matched to your natural teeth
  • Minimally invasive procedure
  • Cost-effective cosmetic solution
  • Immediate results

Smile Makeovers

For comprehensive smile transformation, our complete smile makeovers combine multiple cosmetic procedures to address all your aesthetic concerns. We'll create a customized treatment plan to achieve your ideal smile.

  • Personalized consultation and smile design
  • Digital smile preview technology
  • Combination of multiple procedures
  • Coordinated treatment timeline
  • Life-changing results

Gum Contouring

If you have a "gummy smile" or uneven gum line, gum contouring can reshape your gums to reveal more of your beautiful teeth and create a more balanced, attractive smile.

  • Laser gum sculpting for precision
  • Minimal discomfort and fast healing
  • Create symmetrical gum line
  • Enhance tooth proportions
  • Dramatic smile improvement

Tooth Reshaping & Contouring

Subtle changes can make a big difference. We can reshape and contour your teeth to improve their appearance, smooth rough edges, and create a more harmonious smile.

  • Quick and painless procedure
  • Improve tooth shape and length
  • Smooth chips and irregularities
  • Often completed in one visit
  • Conservative cosmetic enhancement

Straighten your teeth discreetly with Invisalign clear aligners. These virtually invisible aligners gradually move your teeth into the desired position without the look or feel of traditional braces.

  • Clear, removable aligners
  • More comfortable than braces
  • No dietary restrictions
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Predictable results with 3D technology
