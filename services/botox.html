Botox¬Æ for Wrinkle Reduction

Botox is a safe, FDA-approved treatment that temporarily relaxes facial muscles, reducing the appearance of dynamic wrinkles caused by repeated facial expressions.

  • Forehead lines and creases
  • Frown lines between eyebrows (glabellar lines)
  • Crow's feet around the eyes
  • Bunny lines on the nose
  • Results visible within 3-7 days
  • Effects typically last 3-4 months

TMJ & Jaw Pain Relief

Beyond cosmetic benefits, Botox can provide therapeutic relief for temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorders and jaw tension. By relaxing the jaw muscles, we can reduce pain and discomfort.

  • Relief from jaw clenching and grinding
  • Reduction of TMJ pain and headaches
  • Decreased muscle tension
  • Non-surgical pain management
  • Improved quality of life

Gummy Smile Correction

If you show too much gum tissue when you smile, Botox can help. We strategically inject small amounts to relax the upper lip muscles, creating a more balanced, confident smile.

  • Reduce excessive gum display
  • Create more balanced smile proportions
  • Quick and minimally invasive
  • No surgery required
  • Boost your smile confidence

Lip Enhancement

Using dermal fillers, we can add volume to thin lips, define lip borders, and create a fuller, more youthful lip appearance with natural-looking results.

  • Add volume and definition
  • Smooth vertical lip lines
  • Enhance lip symmetry
  • Natural, proportionate results
  • Customized to your facial features

Facial Volume Restoration

As we age, facial volume naturally diminishes. Dermal fillers can restore lost volume, smooth deep folds, and enhance facial contours for a refreshed appearance.

  • Fill nasolabial folds (smile lines)
  • Restore cheek volume
  • Soften marionette lines
  • Enhance chin and jawline definition
  • Immediate, long-lasting results
