General Dentistry

Comprehensive oral health care for the whole family

Complete Oral Health Care

At Alvarez Dental Group, we believe that preventive care is the foundation of excellent oral health. Our comprehensive general dentistry services are designed to keep your teeth and gums healthy for life, while addressing any issues before they become serious problems.

Professional Teeth Cleaning

Regular professional cleanings are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. Our skilled dental hygienists use advanced techniques to remove plaque, tartar, and stains that regular brushing can't reach.

  • Thorough plaque and tartar removal
  • Professional polishing for a brighter smile
  • Fluoride treatments for added protection
  • Personalized home care recommendations

Comprehensive Dental Exams

Our detailed dental examinations help catch potential problems early, when they're easiest and most cost-effective to treat. We use the latest diagnostic technology to ensure nothing is overlooked.

  • Complete oral cancer screening
  • Digital X-rays with minimal radiation
  • Gum disease evaluation and assessment
  • Bite and jaw alignment check
  • Review of medical history and medications

Tooth-Colored Fillings

When cavities occur, we restore your teeth with natural-looking composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your smile. These modern fillings are durable, safe, and virtually invisible.

  • Mercury-free composite materials
  • Color-matched to your natural teeth
  • Strong and long-lasting results
  • Conservative tooth preparation

Preventive Care

Prevention is always better than treatment. We offer a range of preventive services to protect your teeth and help you maintain excellent oral health between visits.

  • Dental sealants to prevent cavities
  • Fluoride treatments for all ages
  • Custom night guards for teeth grinding
  • Sports mouthguards for active patients
  • Nutritional counseling for oral health

Root Canal Therapy

When the inner pulp of a tooth becomes infected or damaged, root canal therapy can save the tooth and relieve pain. Our gentle approach makes the procedure comfortable and effective.

  • Advanced pain management techniques
  • State-of-the-art endodontic equipment
  • Preservation of your natural tooth
  • Same-day emergency appointments available

Dental Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged or missing teeth with custom-crafted crowns and bridges. Our restorations are designed to look natural and function just like your own teeth.

  • High-quality porcelain materials
  • Custom color and shape matching
  • Durable and long-lasting results
  • Same-day crown options available

Extractions

When a tooth cannot be saved or is causing problems, we perform gentle extractions with careful attention to your comfort. We also discuss tooth replacement options to restore your smile.

  • Gentle and pain-free procedures
  • Sedation options for anxious patients
  • Detailed aftercare instructions
  • Options for tooth replacement
