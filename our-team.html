Meet Our Expert Dental Team

Our highly qualified dental professionals bring decades of combined experience in general dentistry, cosmetic procedures, and specialized dental treatments to serve Cambridge families with exceptional care

Dr. Rosario Alvarez

Lead Dentist & Practice Owner

Dr. Alvarez founded Alvarez Dental Group over 20 years ago with a vision to provide exceptional, patient-centered dental care to the Cambridge community. With extensive experience in general and cosmetic dentistry, she brings both expertise and a gentle touch to every patient interaction.

Dr. Maria Rodriguez

General Dentist

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in preventive dental care and cosmetic procedures. She is passionate about helping patients achieve and maintain optimal oral health through comprehensive care and patient education.

Dr. Jennifer Chen

Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Chen brings her artistic eye and technical precision to creating beautiful, natural-looking smiles. She specializes in cosmetic dentistry, including veneers, teeth whitening, and complete smile makeovers.

Sarah Johnson

Licensed Dental Hygienist

Sarah is dedicated to providing professional teeth cleaning and comprehensive oral health education. Her gentle approach and attention to detail ensure every patient has a comfortable and thorough experience.

Lisa Martinez

Dental Assistant & Patient Care Coordinator

Lisa ensures every patient feels welcome and comfortable during their visit. She coordinates care seamlessly and assists in all procedures, making each dental appointment a positive experience.
