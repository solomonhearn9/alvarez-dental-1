Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?
Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of current medications, and any previous dental records or X-rays if available. Arriving 15 minutes early will help us process your paperwork efficiently.
Do you accept dental insurance?
Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will work with your insurance provider to maximize your benefits. We also offer flexible payment plans for treatments not fully covered by insurance.
How often should I have a dental checkup?
We recommend visiting our office every six months for a routine checkup and cleaning. However, depending on your individual oral health needs, we may suggest more frequent visits to ensure optimal dental health.
What dental services do you offer?
We offer comprehensive dental services including general dentistry (cleanings, fillings, root canals), cosmetic dentistry (whitening, veneers), dental implants, Botox treatments, and emergency dental care. Contact us to learn more about any specific service.
Do you offer emergency dental services?
Yes, we understand dental emergencies can happen at any time. Please call our office at (617) 441-0088, and we will do our best to accommodate your urgent dental needs as quickly as possible.
How can I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule an appointment by calling us at (617) 441-0088, using our online booking system, or by filling out the contact form on this page. Our friendly staff will help you find a time that works best for you.
